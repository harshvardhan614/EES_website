About Us

Electrical Engineering Society was initiated as a departmental student chapter of Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT) in 2014 with a vision to work for the betterment and awareness of the field of electrical engineering. EES conducts various technical, managerial, and informal events throughout the academic year for overall development of students. EES also organizes numerous workshops on topics which will help in developing engineering skills amongst students and interactive sessions with well experienced people to provide proper guidance to students. We are also aiming to expand our vision for technical projects and knowledge sharing in this upcoming year.

Aatish

Electrical Engineering Societyhosts AATISH, a techno-managerial festival. We strive for a student's total growth, in which the student is able to explore, learn, develop, innovate, and most significantly, enjoy doing so. AATISH was launched in 2015 with the goal of encouraging students to explore more about electrical engineering through the activities. AATISH comes with a series of technical, management, and informal events all focused on the same theme. The theme for AATISH-2021 was "Carnival," while the theme for AATISH-2019 was "Parallel Universe," and so on. We plan around ten unique activities for students that not only allow them to explore and learn, but also keep them updated about current happenings and trends.Tireless efforts of all the committee members are appreciable for making AATISH successful perennially.
