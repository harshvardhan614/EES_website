    QUIZZIYAPA

    Time duration : 7th Augest, 2021

    Electrical Engineering Society (EES) will be conducting the largest quizzing event of our institute - QUIZIYAPA. It will be a two-round event; qualifying round and final round. The qualifying round will be a computer adaptive Google form round, participants qualifying this round will be competing in the final round. The final round will be live quiz competition on Kahoot along with organizing committee monitoring them through. The quiz will include questions from various domains like politics, current affairs, technology, sports, general awareness, engineering, and our college also. The event will be conducted totally on virtual platforms.
