    Guest lecture on Electric Vehicle by Dr. Madhan Mohan

    Time duration : 18th September, 2021

    Masked Bandits-2022

    The Online Treasure Hunt

    Time duration : 7th January, 2022

    Electrical Engineering Society (EES) will be organizing the online treasure hunt. It will be the first online treasure hunt event of our institute. The primary objective of the event is comforting the incoming first-year batch to the new college atmosphere. The participants will be given a set of hints and a start point. Through tactful and observation skills, they are expected to reach the final point before others for proceeding to the next round. A total of three rounds will be organized, each with increasing difficulty and each round will have new pattern hints and riddles will be asked.

    RECHARGE-2021

    The Annual Recruitment Drive

    Time duration : 13th August, 2021

    Electrical Engineering Society (EES) will be organizing the annual recruitment drive for sophomore executives. The purpose of conducting the event is to introduce the second-year batch about the culture and functioning of EES. It will be followed by interviews to integrate them into our executive committee. After interviews, the selected candidates will be taken into consideration for mentor allotment program along with the core committee members of EES.

    QUIZYAPA-2021

    The Quizzing Event

    Time duration : 7th August, 2021

    (EES) will be conducting the largest quizzing event of our institute - QUIZIYAPA. It will be a two-round event; qualifying round and final round. The qualifying round will be a computer adaptive Google form round, participants qualifying this round will be competing in the final round. The final round will be live quiz competition on Kahoot along with organizing committee monitoring them through. The quiz will include questions from various domains like politics, current affairs, technology, sports, general awareness, engineering, and our college also. The event will be conducted totally on virtual platforms.

    Time duration : 31st January, 2021

    Discharge is the annual orientation of out student chapter.
